Predictive Model

A predictive model that aims to identify how suitable a song is for dancing based on its characteristics such as its acousticness, energy and key. Used Python, Pandas, Sci-kit Learn, Matplotlib, Numpy, Scipy and Seaborn. The final model had an accuracy of 71.2%.

SkillsPython, Pandas, Sci-kit Learn

Project TypeUniversity - Group

Download >Report

Project Brief

To create a predictive model that can identify if a song is likely to be danceable to based on its characteristics.

Details

The dataset which was obtained from Kaggle contains 500 samples and 19 attributes.

Attributes include:

  • Energy - A perceptual measure of intensity and activity (0 to 1)
  • Valence - How happiness inducing the song is (0 to 1)
  • Loudness - Loudness values in dB averaged across the song (-60 to 1)
  • Liveness - The presence of an audience in the song recording (0 to 1)

First, exploratory analysis was conducted to understand the dataset better. Next, various predictive models were built, including logistic regression, decision tree, random forest and support vector machine. Finally, the logistic regression model was selected as it performed the best in terms of accuracy and precision. It achieved an accuracy of 71.2%.
