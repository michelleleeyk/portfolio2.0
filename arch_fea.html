Finite Element Analysis

Designed a 3D model of a bicycle tandem frame and conducted stress, frequency and fatigue simulations on it in order to identify the ideal frame. Simulations were conducted in SolidWorks using the Finite Element Method. The benchmark used was a natural frequency of more than 30Hz and a lifespan of at least one million loading cycles.
